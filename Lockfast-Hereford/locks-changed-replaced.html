Locks Changed, Replaced or Repaired

Residential Lock Repairs and Replacement

Lock replacement means changing the existing locks to new ones while rekeying, on the other hand, also means keeping the locks unchanged – minus a few adjustments – and only changing the keys. Locks are replaced when there is an issue with the lock, such as when they are broken, damaged, or malfunctioning. Locks and keys work in pairs and the system becomes compromised if either or both show signs of wear and tear, which is why it is best to have them replaced ASAP in such situations. Locks are rekeyed when key control is lost, such as when you lose them or they get stolen, in which case, there is a high chance of them falling in the wrong hands and jeopardising your home security. Rekeying works best in these situations, rendering the old, lost, and stolen ones completely useless. Both procedures, rekeying in particular, require an in-depth understanding of various locking mechanisms and using the right tools to getting the job done.

Don’t Replace It – Repair It:

Whether you’ve got a key stuck or cut off in the lock, if the mechanism inside feels a bit stiff, or if there are some obvious signs of damage, we can offer door and window lock repair services for all types of locks including mortice, UPVC cylinders, window locks and night latches. Faulty locks that are left for too long often get worse, and can put the security of your home at serious risk. It’s always better to prevent than react. We can help you resolve in a timely manner. If you were to suffer a break-in, your insurer may not pay out if they determine that a faulty lock contributed to the intruder’s ability to enter your home. In some circumstances the lock may be beyond repair, in which case we will offer a safe, appropriate and cost-effective replacement solution, with a warranty. However, where a lock can be fixed for less than the cost of replacing it, we will always endeavour to fix it for you. Common Problems with Shops and Offices

Common issues with shop and office doors are when the door speed is too fast or slamming very hard against the door frame or where the door is not in line preventing the door from locking or being unlocked. An adjustment is sometimes all that is needed to allow the doors to function as they should however sometimes there may be a need to repair or replace the door closer, lock or cylinder.
